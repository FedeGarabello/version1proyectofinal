About Us

Hillsong Young & Free (also known as Hillsong Y&F or simply Y&F) is an Australian contemporary worship music group from Sydney, Australia, where they started making Christian music in 2012 at Hillsong Church. They have released three studio albums, We Are Young & Free (2013), Youth Revival Acoustic (2017), and III (2018) as well as two live albums, Youth Revival (2016) and III Live (2018). Additionally, the group have released two extended plays, This Is Living (2015) and We Are Young & Free (The Remixes) - EP (2015).
